#### 系统介绍
打造国内领先的无人机驾驶员培训平台，融合“理论+实操+证书”全流程，为学员提供一站式学习体验，为学校提供高效管理工具。

 **1．项目背景与目标** 

随着无人机技术的快速发展，其在农业植保、电力巡检、航拍测绘、应急救援等领域的应用日益广泛。根据中国民航局（CAAC）规定，操作一定重量以上的无人机必须持有相应等级的驾驶员执照。因此，专业、高效、规范的无人机驾驶员培训需求迅速增长。
然而，当前无人机培训存在以下痛点：


 - 培训资源分散，缺乏统一管理平台

 - 理论学习依赖纸质教材，效率低

 - 实操课程训练记录混乱，退费纠纷频繁

 - 学员进度无法实时追踪

 - 学校运营依赖人工，数据统计滞后



本项目旨在建设一个集理论教学、实操训练、证书管理、学校运营于一体的综合性无人机培训平台，为学员提供一站式学习服务，为培训学校提供数字化管理工具，推动无人机教育培训行业规范化、智能化发展。

 **2．平台定位** 

打造国内领先的无人机驾驶员培训平台，融合“理论+实操+证书”全流程，为学员提供一站式学习体验，为学校提供高效管理工具。

面向人群：


- 无人机初学者、爱好者

- 从事无人机相关行业的从业人员

- 各类无人机培训学校、飞行学院


核心功能：


- 在线理论学习（视频+题库+模拟考试）

- 实操课程预约与管理

- 学员进度跟踪与数据分析

- 学校工作台与运营管理

### English version introduction
### System Introduction
To create a leading domestic drone pilot training platform that integrates the entire process of "theory+practice+certification", providing students with a one-stop learning experience and efficient management tools for schools.

**1. Project background and objectives**

With the rapid development of drone technology, its applications in agricultural crop protection, power inspection, aerial surveying, emergency rescue and other fields are becoming increasingly widespread. According to the regulations of the Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC), operating drones of a certain weight or more requires holding a corresponding level of driver's license. Therefore, the demand for professional, efficient, and standardized drone pilot training is rapidly increasing. However, there are currently the following pain points in drone training:

Training resources are scattered and lack a unified management platform

Theoretical learning relies on paper textbooks, which is inefficient

The training records of practical courses are chaotic, and there are frequent disputes over refunds

Student progress cannot be tracked in real-time

School operations rely on manual labor, and data statistics are lagging behind

The aim of this project is to build a comprehensive drone training platform that integrates theoretical teaching, practical training, certificate management, and school operation, providing one-stop learning services for students, digital management tools for training schools, and promoting the standardized and intelligent development of the drone education and training industry.

**2. Platform positioning**

To create a leading domestic drone pilot training platform that integrates the entire process of "theory+practice+certification", providing students with a one-stop learning experience and efficient management tools for schools.

Target audience:

Beginners and enthusiasts of drones

Employees engaged in the drone related industry

Various drone training schools and flight academies

Core functions:

Online theoretical learning (video+question bank+mock exam)

Appointment and Management of Practical Courses

Student progress tracking and data analysis

School Workbench and Operations Management
